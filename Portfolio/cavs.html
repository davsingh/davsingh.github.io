The Cavaliers' Team

The 2016 NBA Champion Cleveland Cavaliers are lead by the Future Hall of Famer, widely regarded as one of the best players in NBA history, Lebron James. Making his shocking return to his hometown city of Cleveland, he has brought them to 3 straight NBA Finals appearances while appearing in 8 consecutive himself. This year he is paired with Kevin Love and others as they contend for another championship. Lebron James is averaging 27.4 points per game , 9.1 assists per game, and 7.4 rebounds per game. Kevin Love is his all-star sidekick averaging 17.5ppg, 9.4rpg.

Others: JR Smith: 8.3ppg, Tristan Thompson: 6.prpg, Kyle Korver: 9.3ppg, Iman Shumpert: 7.5ppg, Deron Williams: 7.5ppg
