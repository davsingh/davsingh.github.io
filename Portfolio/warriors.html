The Warriors' Team

The Golden State Warriors in the Bay Area of California are reigning NBA Champions and have won 2 of the last 3, all facing the Cleveland Cavaliers. With the NBA-infamous acquisition of Kevin Durant, the Warriors have become a powerhouse in the NBA. Lead by an overpowering core of Stephen Curry, Draymond Green, Kevin Durant, and Klay Thompson, the Warriors are just too much for most teams to handle. Curry is averaging 26.4 points per game, 5.1 rebounds, and 6.1 assists along with Durants 26.5, 6.9, 5.4, numbers.
