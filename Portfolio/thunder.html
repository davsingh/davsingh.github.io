The Thunder Team

The Oklahoma City Thunder are a relatively small market team in the NBA, but their spectacular play has earned them fans all over the world! Lead by their uber-athletic MVP point guard, Russell Westbrook, this team has secured a spot in the 2018 NBA Playoffs. They have a big rivalry with another team represented in this site, the Golden State Warriors. Before the start of last season, Oklahoma City's other star player, Kevin Durant left the team to join the Warriors. Russell Westbrook responded by averaging a triple double (double digits in 3 statistical categories). This year he is averaging 25.6 points, 10.1 assists, and 9.9 assists per game. Paul George, another star player added to the team this year - averaging 21.6ppg, 5.7rpg, 3.4apg
